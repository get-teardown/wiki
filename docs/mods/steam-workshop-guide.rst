Browsing the Workshop
=====================

The Steam Workshop allows creators to share maps, tools, and vehicles.

* **Most Popular:** Check the "Most Popular" tab for high-quality, community-vetted mods.
* **Categories:** Filter by Maps, Tools, or Vehicles.

Managing Mods
=============

In the game's main menu:

1. Click **Mods**.
2. You will see a list of your subscribed items.
3. Toggle them **On** or **Off**.
4. Some mods may conflict; if the game crashes, try disabling recently added mods.

Featured Mods
=============

* *Physics Gun:* Picking up and throwing objects.
* *Structural Integrity:* Visualize stress on buildings.
* *Minigun:* High fire-rate destruction.
